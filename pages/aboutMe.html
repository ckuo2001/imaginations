    • Hello! I am Christina and I am a junior majoring in Human-Centered Design & Engineering at Univeristy of Washington.

      I love imagining me creating different kinds of apps and websites, and that's why this website is called imaginations!

      I thought I would major in Computer Science as I enjoy creating apps and websites; however, I couldn't bring out my creativity in Computer Science, which made me doubt myself: is Computer Science the best match for me?

      While being lost about my life plan, Human-Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE) came into my life. Taking the intro class of HCDE, I already knew that it is the best choice for me because I can think creatively and design things as much as I want.

      I'm glad that I chose HCDE because I love designing, and I know this is the right track.
