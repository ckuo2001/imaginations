Introduction

As a college student, I have noticed the importance of study groups. Studying with other people not only helps students to increase the efficiency of studying but also reduces the anxiety on exams and assignments. However, there is only a few social media and apps that are for studying purpose for students nowadays. As a result, the idea of Study Together popped up in my mind and I decided to design it!

Study Together is an app that helps students, especially college students, to find their study groups or study mates online.

After giving a little brief of my app, it’s time to tell the process of designing the app!

Empathy

I started the designing process by researching why people like to find study groups, how study groups help students, and what kind of study groups. Moreover, I did some research on the apps that usually are used as online study groups, such as Discord and Slack.

These are my notes from the research:

And these are my findings:

  • It is better to have the same study habits, level of knowledge, and preferred language with people in the same study group
  • Most people like to have different channels or places for group chat and direct messages
  • The group chat or direct message should allow users to voice call and video call
Define

define users' needs from the research

  • They need a group of people who study at the same school and same class to study together
  • They need to have common study habits and knowledge levels
  • Meeting once a week at least to keep up with the school
  • They need a group leader, or they can be assigned to different roles
  • They should have some entertainment besides studying
Ideate
Features
  • Home

    where people can find and see study groups

  • Post

    where people can create study groups and posting them

  • Direct Message

    where users can message another person privately

  • Group Chat

    where study group members can chat. Also, a place to receive the rewards and notifications

  • Profile

    where has the user’s name, school, introduction, a list of interests, joined groups, and study habits

Wireframes
High-Fidelity Wireframes
Home - Looking for Study Groups

Users can see the study groups that fit their interest on the Home page. The filter helps users to find the study groups that match them the most.

Group Chat and Direct Message

Users can chat with each other by the chat functions. They also receive the rewards and notifications about weekly meetings from the group chat.

Posting a Study Group

The user can go to the Post page to post a study group by entering some group information. The study group will be visible after the user posted it, and a group chat will also be created automatically.

Profile

On the Profile page, users can see the categories they are interested in and also the groups they have joined.
